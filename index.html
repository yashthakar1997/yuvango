About NGO
YUVA - the NGO is a Non - Profit Organisation being run by young individuals with a vision to enhance the good qualities lying within the Youth of India for a constructive and better future of the Nation. Embracing the theme of Youth Empowerment and keeping the moral values and ethics in centre; the NGO will prove to be a benchmark in training the Young citizens especially the students to make them understand their need for the society and thereby serve the Nation in whole.
